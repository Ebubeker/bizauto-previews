What "hygge" means here.

Hygge is a Danish word for the warmth of being comfortable and at ease. We named the house after it because that is the one thing we want you to feel. Slow mornings in the garden, a kettle that is always on, and a bed that feels like your own after the first night.

It is a quiet place at the end of a dead-end street, yet the centre of the city is a fifteen-minute walk away. Come in, drop your bag, and let the trip slow down a little.

Simple, comfortable rooms.

The rooms are not flashy, and we like them that way. Each one is bright and clean, with a comfortable bed, air conditioning for the summer, and the kind of quiet you only get at the end of a dead-end street.

Bathrooms are shared and kept spotless. There is free WiFi throughout, a place to leave your bag, and parking on request when you let us know ahead of time.
