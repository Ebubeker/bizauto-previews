More a home
than a hostel

Most people remember two things about the Golden Rooster: the garden where everyone ends up in the evening, and Franc, who welcomes you in, shows you around, and points you to the best of the city.

It is a big, bright old house with a fully equipped kitchen, two comfy lounges with board games and a TV, and a little bar. Easy to arrive at on your own, hard to leave without a few new friends.

  • Enclosed garden
  • Shared kitchen
  • Free walking tour
  • Film nights
