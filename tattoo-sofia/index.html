Less noise,
more detail.

Sofia works in fine-line and micro-realism, the kind of tattoo where the meaning sits in the small things. Thin, deliberate lines. Soft black-and-grey shading. Nothing louder than it needs to be.

Every piece starts with a conversation. You bring the reference, the story, the spot on the body. Sofia draws it from scratch, just for you, then refines it together until it reads right at arm's length and up close.

She tattoos out of NUVVU Studio in central Tirana, a calm room with a steady hand and no rush. Clients come from across the city and abroad for work that holds up over the years.

02

What Sofia tattoos

  1. /01

    Fine-line

    Thin, single-needle work for delicate designs, small script and detail that stays clean as it heals.

  2. /02

    Black & grey realism

    Soft gradients and depth in greyscale, for portraits, animals and objects that read like a photograph.

  3. /03

    Micro-realism

    Realism scaled down. Fine, intricate pieces packed into a small space without losing the detail.

  4. /04

    Custom lettering

    Hand-drawn script and type, spaced and weighted to sit naturally on the body.

  5. /05

    Custom design

    Have an idea but not the image yet? Sofia draws the whole concept with you, from sketch to stencil.

How a piece comes together

01

Consultation

Tell Sofia the idea, the placement and the size. She talks through what works on skin and what to expect, with no pressure to book on the spot.

02

Custom design

She draws your piece from scratch and sends it back for notes. You adjust together until the linework and shading sit exactly where they should.

03

Your session

On the day, the stencil goes on, the timing is yours, and the work is done unhurried at NUVVU Studio. Aftercare guidance comes with it.
